The Top 4 Ice Cream Parlors in Hattiesburg

Number 4: Baskin Robbins

There's not much wrong with Baskin Robbins, they are simply a mass produced, far reaching chain.

Number 3: Asian Cafe

Asian Cafe has a nice side counter where they make unique rolled ice cream. Not the best thing ever, but they win a spot over a massive chain.

Number 2: Marble Slab

Good ice cream with unlimited "mix-ins" which is their version of toppings.

Number 1: Hershey's

They win without anything flashy. Just amazing ice cream at a cheap price.
